Transforming Lives Through E-education

This is a new solution designed to close the learning gap during this COVID-19 pandemic,
the E-learning is an Online, Mobile, and Offline tech platform enabling high quality, flexible learning 24/7

Our Mission

To enable continuous access to E-education for children, youth, and teachers around the world and drive improved learning outcomes through high-quality,
portable education to support their entry into other education or opportunity pathways, including formal education

 COVID-19 Restrictions

The E-education platform has undergone rapid expansion to facilitate country-level curriculum for children and youth whose schools have been forced to close due to COVID-19. The platform will also provide key resources to teachers and educators.
